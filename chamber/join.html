NP Membership Benefits

Support community impact while gaining chamber access.

  • No membership fee for qualifying nonprofit organizations.
  • Invitation to chamber networking events and nonprofit roundtables.
  • Access to selected training workshops and partner resources.
  • Directory listing that helps members discover your mission.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Get started with reliable support for your next stage of growth.

  • Business directory listing and invitation to chamber mixers.
  • Member pricing for workshops, seminars, and select events.
  • Monthly chamber newsletter mentions and resource updates.
  • Eligibility to participate in vendor fairs and community showcases.

Silver Membership Benefits

Increase your exposure and unlock stronger member advantages.

  • Everything in Bronze, plus larger event discounts.
  • Featured promotion opportunities in chamber communications.
  • Priority access to business development training and panels.
  • Preferred placement in selected networking and trade events.

Gold Membership Benefits

Lead from the front with our most visible and rewarding chamber package.

  • Everything in Silver, plus premium spotlight opportunities.
  • Top placement options for homepage or campaign advertising.
  • Exclusive invitations to executive briefings and partner receptions.
  • Highest event discounts and priority sponsorship consideration.
